This years 2018-2019 classes have been instructed to come up with a project that involves all six classes. With this, the classes have come up with a 911 Mock Scenario. This 911 scenario represents a crime scene from start to finish. First, an active shooter shoots two people and then flees the seen. Then, an eye witness calls 911 which then dispatches the local police and emergency ambulance. After the crime scence is situated and cleaned up, the scenario is fast forwards two weeks to get checked up on.
