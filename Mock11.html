The Livingston Parish Literacy and Technology center is located in Walker, Louisiana. This school offers many classes that any high school juniors or seniors in Livingson Parish can enroll in. The classes offered at LPLTC are Automotive Service Tech 1 & 2, Computer Service Tech 1 & 2, Criminal Justice, Engineering, EMT, and Pre Nursing.
